HVNGRY for even more? Become the change. If you've got a piece of work you'd like to share with us, you can email it into our submissions box: submissions@hvngrymag.com. Remember to let us know your name, a little excerpt/description of your piece - and if there's photos - remember to include the source of where you got it from. We'll get back to you as soon as we can - please be patient! Feel free to let us know if you'd like us to link to your website/tumblr/twitter/blog/etc.

What kind of works are we interested in? Any that catches our eye, really. Whether it be an article, illustration, photography series, video, reviews, stories, poetry, music...  We're here to share whatever your passion is, so don't be shy! As always, if you report on something sensitive you can always, always ask to remain anonymous. We will always respect your wishes when it comes to your work. Don't even want to reveal your identity to our editors? Submit completely anonymously <3

Got ideas for a future column? A wonderful person to interview? Let us know!

If you have any questions for us -- whether they be about health, beauty, sex, school, life -- drop us a line in our ask box: ask@hvngrymag.com. As always, we can send messages to us anonymously through our ask box on our website. Try not to sell us anything or your message will be automatically filtered out ;)
